Privacy Policy

Last updated: 22 June 2026

This policy explains what information is — and isn't — collected when you use the Maya GLB I/O website (annomodtutorials.github.io/maya-glb-io), download the software, or contact us. Maya GLB I/O is a free, open-source project maintained by LordJacko ("we", "us", "our").

1. The website

This website is a static site hosted on GitHub Pages. We do not set cookies, and we do not run third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking scripts. The page loads no external scripts or fonts. As the host, GitHub may automatically log standard technical information (such as your IP address and browser type) for security and operational purposes; this processing is covered by GitHub's privacy statement.

2. Downloading the software

The plugin is distributed through GitHub Releases and through Gumroad. If you download via Gumroad, Gumroad collects the information you provide at checkout (such as your email address) and processes it under Gumroad's privacy policy. Downloads via GitHub are governed by GitHub's privacy statement. We do not receive payment information, and the plugin is free.

3. Contacting us by email

If you email saltercreektools@gmail.com — for support, bug reports, or any other reason — we receive your email address and the contents of your message, and we use them solely to respond to you. We do not add you to any marketing list and we do not share your message with third parties.

4. What we don't do

  • We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data.
  • We do not build advertising profiles or track you across other sites.
  • We do not collect data through the website beyond what is described above.

5. Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal data you have sent us (for example, an email you sent to our support address) by writing to saltercreektools@gmail.com. For data held by GitHub or Gumroad, please refer to their respective privacy policies.

6. Children

This website and software are intended for general/professional 3D and game-development use and are not directed at children under 13.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "last updated" date above reflects the current version.

8. Contact

Questions about privacy? Email saltercreektools@gmail.com.
